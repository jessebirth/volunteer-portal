🔒 Privacy & Data Disclosure
By using this portal, you understand and agree to the following:
  • Your personal information (name, email address, date of birth, and identification documents) will be collected and stored securely within AdventHealth's environment.
  • Your information will be shared only with AdventHealth Polk County Market Volunteer Services staff for the purpose of processing your volunteer application.
  • No documents will be transmitted by email. All files are stored in a compliant, access-controlled document library.
  • If you are approved as a volunteer, your data will be entered into AdventHealth's systems to create your official volunteer record.
  • Adult applicants will be subject to a criminal background check as required by AdventHealth policy.
  • Your data is governed in accordance with AdventHealth's privacy policy and applicable federal and state regulations.
  • You may contact WFD.VolunteerServicesPolk@AdventHealth.com at any time with questions about how your information is used.

Your full journey to becoming a volunteer
1
Application review
Coordinator reviews your documents, forms, and background check results (adults: 3–5 business days).
2
Health screening form sent to you
If approved, the system generates a PDF with your name, campus, cost center, and two requested free health screening exams.
3
Clinic visit for free health screening
Bring your generated PDF to Centra Care. The clinic conducts the two exams at no cost to you.
4
Microsoft Teams interview scheduled
Once screening results are received, a Teams meeting invitation is automatically generated for your coordinator interview.
5
NES submission & OPID assigned
After the interview, your data is submitted to the Non-Employee System (NES) and an OPID is requested and assigned.
6
Visit HR office — photo & volunteer badge
With your OPID assigned, you will be invited to the HR office to take your photo and receive your official volunteer badge.
7
Volunteer record created in UKG & Workday
Your official volunteer profile is created in both UKG and Workday — AdventHealth's HR systems of record.
8
In-person orientation & department placement
You are scheduled for in-person orientation and notified of your department placement and shift details.
9
Ongoing shift management & volunteer maintenance
Your shifts are managed through the volunteer portal. Welcome to the AdventHealth Polk County Market Volunteer team!

📋 Application Status Definitions & Required Actions
StatusMeaningYour Action
New Submission received from Microsoft Forms. No coordinator action taken yet. Open the record in the Volunteer Applications list and begin review. Verify all required documents are present in Volunteer Application Attachments.
In Review Coordinator has opened and is actively reviewing the application. Complete document review, verify ID, confirm campus, check background check status (adults). Update status when decision is reached.
Flagged — Missing Docs One or more required documents are absent from Volunteer Application Attachments. Contact the candidate at their registered email. List the specific missing documents. Record the contact attempt in the Notes field. Do not advance status until resolved.
Cleared All documents verified, background check (if applicable) complete, health screen cleared. Candidate is approved to proceed. Generate the Centra Care health screening PDF. Update Centra Care Health Screens list. Trigger Teams interview scheduling via Power Automate flow PA – Centra Care Health Screen Request.
Not Approved Application did not meet requirements after full review. Send a notification to the candidate. Document the specific reason in the Notes field. Do not delete the record — it must remain for audit purposes.
Onboarded Candidate fully processed: OPID assigned, badge issued, UKG and Workday records created. Confirm NES submission is complete, OPID is recorded in the Volunteer Applications list, HR badge visit is documented, and both UKG and Workday records are created. Schedule in-person orientation.
✅ Post-Approval Checklist (in order)
  • Set application status to Cleared in the Volunteer Applications SharePoint list
  • Generate health screening PDF using campus template (HOF or LW) — cost center and account number auto-fill from Centra Care billing reference below
  • Send health screening PDF to candidate and create entry in the Centra Care Health Screens list
  • Confirm candidate attended Centra Care and update Cleared field to Yes in the Centra Care Health Screens list
  • Schedule Microsoft Teams interview via Power Automate flow PA – Centra Care Health Screen Request
  • After interview: submit candidate data to NES and request OPID. Record OPID in Volunteer Applications list once received
  • Invite candidate to HR office for photo and badge. Update BadgeIssued field when complete
  • Create volunteer record in UKG and Workday. Update corresponding fields in the Volunteer Applications list
  • Schedule in-person orientation and confirm department placement
  • Set application status to Onboarded to trigger the welcome email via Power Automate flow PA – Volunteer Application Intake

📁 SharePoint Structure (Canonical Names)
NameTypePurpose
Volunteer Intake – Source Forms (Controlled)Document LibraryRead-only. Stores official PDF/DOCX templates: Teen Application, Adult Application, Centra Care Health Screen forms. Authoritative source for Compliance and Audit.
