Your full journey to becoming a volunteer
1
Application review
Coordinator reviews your documents, forms, and background check results (adults: 3–5 business days).
2
Health screening form sent to you
If approved, the system generates a PDF with your name, campus, cost center, and two requested free health screening exams. You will receive this by email.
3
Clinic visit for free health screening
Bring your generated PDF to the clinic. The clinic conducts the two requested exams at no cost to you.
4
Microsoft Teams interview scheduled
Once your screening results are received, a Teams meeting invitation is automatically generated for your interview with a volunteer coordinator.
5
NES submission & OPID assigned
After the interview, your data is submitted to the Non-Employee System (NES) and an OPID — your unique non-employee identifier — is requested and assigned.
6
Visit HR office — photo & volunteer badge
With your OPID in hand, you will be invited to the HR office to have your photo taken and receive your official AdventHealth Polk volunteer badge.
7
Volunteer record created in UKG & Workday
Your official volunteer profile is created in both UKG and Workday — AdventHealth's HR systems of record.
8
In-person orientation & department placement
You are scheduled for in-person orientation and notified of your department placement and shift details.
9
Ongoing shift management & volunteer maintenance
Your shifts are managed through the volunteer portal. Welcome to the AdventHealth Polk Volunteer team!
